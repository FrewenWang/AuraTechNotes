



## norm()

**对于Vector**，norm返回的是向量的二范数，即

![img](https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5C%7C%5Cmathbf%7Bx%7D%5C%7C_%7B2%7D%3D%5Csqrt%7B%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7D%20x_%7Bi%7D%5E%7B2%7D%7D)

例如：

```c++
Vector2d vec(3.0,4.0);
cout << vec.norm() << endl;	//输出5
```
